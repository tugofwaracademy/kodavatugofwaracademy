Balekuttira Family Cup

2025 Edition

Year: 2025
Host Family: Balekuttira

Balekuttira Okka, Mythadi successfully organised the Fourth Kodava Tug of War Festival in 2025 at SMS School Ground, Arameri. The event brought together families in a spectacular display of strength, unity, and Kodava tradition.
