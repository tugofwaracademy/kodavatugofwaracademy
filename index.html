About the Academy

VISION

The Kodava Tug of War Academy envisions a future where the traditional Kodava family tug of war festival thrives as a vibrant cultural and sporting heritage. By nurturing young talent, documenting traditions, and promoting community participation, the academy aims to strengthen Kodava identity while introducing this unique tradition to national and international audiences.

MISSION

The mission of the Kodava Tug of War Academy is to safeguard and promote the traditional Kodava family tug of war festival by encouraging community participation, nurturing young talent, documenting the cultural significance of the tradition, and organizing training and competitions that strengthen Kodava identity and ensure the continuity of this heritage sport for generations to come.

CORE VALUES

Heritage Preservation

We are committed to protecting and promoting the rich cultural traditions of the Kodava community, ensuring that the historic tug of war festival continues to thrive.

Community Unity

The academy celebrates the spirit of togetherness among Kodava families and villages, strengthening bonds through shared participation and cultural pride.

Respect for Tradition

We honor the customs, values, and rituals associated with the Kodava festival while passing them faithfully to future generations.

Youth Engagement

We encourage young people to learn, participate in, and take pride in their cultural heritage through sport and community activities.

Excellence in Sport

We promote discipline, teamwork, and athletic excellence in tug of war while maintaining the cultural essence of the traditional event.

Cultural Education

We aim to document, research, and share knowledge about Kodava traditions so that the wider community understands and appreciates this unique heritage.

Our Recognitions

Bharatiya book of Records

Officially recognized for organizing one of the largest family-oriented sporting events in the region.

Karnataka Book of Records

Honored for maximum participation of distinct family lineages in a single traditional sports tournament.

Community Milestone

Successfully running the tournament for consecutive years with growing community engagement.
