Bottolanda Family Cup

2024 Edition

Back to Events
Year: 2024
Host Family: Bottolanda

Bottolanda Okka, Napoklu successfully organised the Third Kodava Tug of War Festival in 2024 at Junior College Ground, Napoklu. The event brought together Kodava families in a spirited display of strength and community pride.

Tournament Results

Men's Category

1st Place:Maletira Okka, Kuklooru
2nd Place:Machimanda Okka, Kavadi

Women's Category

1st Place:Kandanda Okka, Kolakeri, Napoklu
2nd Place:Ajjamada Okka, Kurchi, Beeruga
